Who We Are

The first educational platform in the Arab world specialized in medical sciences

Yotta Medical School launched in 2019 to provide practical, high-impact learning for graduates and professionals in medicine, pharmacy, dentistry, veterinary medicine, physiotherapy, and nursing.

Our programs are delivered by experienced doctors, pharmacists, scientists, and field experts who transform complex knowledge into clear, career-ready skills.

We are committed to helping learners bridge the gap between university education and real market needs through flexible, professional courses that can be attended anywhere and at any time.

EBU Accredited

Trusted learning standards with real professional value.

Vision & Mission

Built to shape confident professionals for the future of healthcare

Our Vision

To become the most trusted medical education destination in the Arab world by delivering modern, high-quality learning experiences that elevate careers and healthcare outcomes.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between academic knowledge and labor market needs through applied courses, expert mentorship, and practical skill-building for students and working professionals.
