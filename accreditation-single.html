Accreditation Details

The cooperation agreement between Ajman University and E3mel Business aims to prepare, develop, and train the professional talent required by the labor market, while helping learners through high-quality education and training services.

This accreditation reflects a clear strategic direction toward building educational partnerships that enhance training content in terms of quality, credibility, and professional value.
